2019-06-22

If语句的规则：

  1. 每一个if语句必须包含一个else；
  2. 如果这个else永远都不应该被执行（比如本身无意义），则必须在else语句后面实用一个叫做die的函数；
  3. If语句的嵌套不要超过2层，最好尽量保持只有1层。也就是说，如果你在if里又嵌套了一个if，那需要把第二个if移到另一个函数里；
  4. If语句当作段落来对待，其中的每一个if，elif，else组合就和一个段落的句子组合一样，这种组合的最前面和最后面留一个空行作区分；
  5. 布尔测试应该很简单，如果复杂，就需要将它们的运算事先放到一个变量里，并为变量取一个好名字。

循环的原则：

  1. while循环是永不停止的循环，务必注意；
  2. 其他类型实用for循环，尤其是循环的对象数量固定或有限的情况。

调试debug的小技巧

  1. debugger类似全身扫描，不会根据特定方面得到信息；
  2. 最好的调试方法是每一步都print，从而检查哪里有问题；
  3. 写一点运行一点，不要等脚本太长了才运行。

作业：
结合列表、函数、模组等设计一个脚本游戏（类似ex35的选择游戏）。
我的作业代码：

from sys import argv #导入argv模组
from sys import exit #导入exit模组
import random #导入随机数random
choice_game, first, second, third = argv #定义script，ice，forest，water为argv参数

life = 100

first = input("Name the first choice:")
second = input("Name the second choice:")
third = input("Name the third choice:")

print("Now we start the game, the game is calle:", choice_game)
print("Your first choice is:", first)
print("Your second choice is:", second)
print("Your third choice is:", third)
print("At the beginning, you have %d life." % life)

def first_choice():
    print("""The first choice is full of ice.
    It's the ice world.
    Which choice do you want to face?
    The 1, 2 or 3?
    You can change to other choices.""")
		
    choice = input("Your choice:")
		
    if choice == "1":
        choice_1 = "ice wolf"
        print("You are facing the %s, you can survive but your life will lose 50." % choice_1)
        print("Your life now is %d" % (life - 50))
    elif choice == "2":
        choice_2 = "ice bear"
        print("You are facing the %s, you can survive but your life will lose 70." % choice_2)
        print("Your life now is %d" % (life - 70))
    elif choice == "3":
        choice_3 = 'no food'
        print("You are facing the %s, your life will lose 100 and you can't survive." % choice_3)
        print("Your life now is %d" % (life - 100))
        dead("There's no food to eat, You can't survive in the %s." % first)
    elif choice == "change to the second choice":
        second_choice()
    elif choice == "change to the third choice":
        third_choice()
    else:
        dead("Man, learn to type a number.")
				
def second_choice():
    print("""The second choice is full of tree.
    It's the forest world.
    Which choice do you want to face?
    The 1, 2, 3 or 4?
    You can change to other choices.""")
		
    while True:
        choice = input("Your choice:")
				
        if choice == "1":
            choice_1 = "forest deer"
            print("You are facing the %s, you can survive and your life will add 70." % choice_1)
            print("Your life now is %d" % (life + 70))
        elif choice == "2":
            choice_2 = "forest rabit"
            print("You are facing the %s, you can survive and your life will and 20." % choice_2)
            print("Your life now is %d" % (life + 20))
        elif choice == "3":
            choice_3 = "forest tiger"
            print("You are facing the %s, you can survive but your life will lose 80." % choice_3)
            print("Your life now is %d" % (life - 80))
        elif choice == "4":
            choice_4 = "no food"
            print("You are facing the %s, your life will lose 100 and you can't survive." % choice_4)
            print("Your life now is %d" % (life - 100))
            dead("There's no food to eat, You can't survive in the %s." % second)
        elif choice == "change to the first choice":
            first_choice()
        elif choice == "change to the third choice":
            third_choice()
        else:
            dead("Man, learn to type a number.")
						
def third_choice():
    print("""The third choice is full of water.
    It's the water world.
    Which choice do you want to face?
    The bird, fish, monster or else?
    You can change to other choices.""")
		
    fish_escaped = False
		
    while True:
        choice = input("Your choice:")
				
        if choice == "bird":
            choice_1 = "water bird"
            print("You are facing the %s, you can survive and your life will add 30." % choice_1)
            print("Your life now is %d" % (life + 30))
        elif choice == "fish" and not fish_escaped:
            choice_2 = "water fish"
            print("You are facing the %s." % choice_2)
            print("The fish hasn't escaped, you get the fish to eat. Your life will add 50.")
            print("Your life now is %d" % (life + 50))
            fish_escaped = True
        elif choice == "fish" and fish_escaped:
            print("The fish has escaped, you can't get the fish to eat. Your life will lose 100.")
            print("Your life now is %s" % (life - 100))
            dead("You have nothing to eat, so you die.")
        elif choice == "monster":
            choice_3 = "monster"
            print("You are facing the %s" % choice_3)
            print("You can't beat the monster")
            dead("The monster eat you.")
        elif choice == "change to the first choice":
            first_choice()
        elif choice == "change to the second choice":
            second_choice()
        else:
            print("Try another choice.")
            dead("Game over. Try again.")
						
def dead(why):
    print(why, "Good job!")
    exit(0)
		
def start():
    print("""You are starting the game.
    There are three choices in the game.
    Which one do you want to take?
    the first choice, the second choice or the third choice?""")
		
    choice = input(">")
		
    if choice == "first choice":
        first_choice()
    elif choice == "second choice":
        second_choice()
    elif choice == "third choice":
        third_choice()
    else:
        dead("You need to make a choice to hunt or you will die.")
				
start()
